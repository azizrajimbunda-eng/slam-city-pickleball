◆ National Pickle Open ◆ Doubles Final · Center Court
Aim with WASD as you swing · J/K/L/U

Broadcast view, from behind the baseline — just like the real thing. Four players, a live third-shot drop, a kitchen dink battle, then someone speeds it up and the hands fly. Hit 🎮 Take Control to grab the near-side red paddle. WASD to move; press J dink · K drive · L smash to swing. It's about timing: line up the strike bracket over the ball and swing as it turns green — good positioning = a PERFECT shot; sloppy timing pops up a sitter the CPU will pounce on. Your AI partner covers the rest. Slow by default (real pickleball) — it bursts fast on drives, smashes and net battles. Click the screen first so it hears your keys.

◆ How to Play ◆

Doubles pickleball, broadcast view — you're the near-side red paddle

🕹️

Drag anywhere in the joystick zone (bottom-left) to move

🏓

Tap DINK / DRIVE / SMASH / LOB to swing — time it as the ball crosses your strike zone
